About Me

Hi my name is James.
After having worked in various jobs over the last few years, working as a camera operator, in community digital radio and supermarkets. I discovered Codenation through a recruitment session.
I have a drive and determination to learn new things and within the ever evolving IT environment I discovered a place where I can learn and adapt to the world of coding. Entering Codenation on the first day I came with little to no knowledge of how computer science worked, I found it to be a steep learning curve, however with every passing day my understanding slowly built and created a rewarding experience.
